// tag::EN[]
==== Sum

Sum data is data that can be one of several distinct, but fixed types.
Also known as *discriminated union*, *dijoint union*,
*mixed data* or *or data*.

Example:

A *geometric shape* is one of the following:

- a square, or
- a circle, or
- a triangle
